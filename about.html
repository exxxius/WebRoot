Hello my friends!

 

Well, I’m really motivated & passionate about music as much as you can’t even imagine, I don’t think any of you have got tortured by the government & forced for leave your home country because of music, but I did & it’s a long story that I can tell you about it later... So you may get the point of how serious I am about music in my life. I’m a 30 years old guy, apparently I don’t have a Rock-star appearance. I’ve been playing Guitar since 15 years ago. I studied World/Classical music at Tehran Conservatory and Jazz/Rock music at Vienna (Austria) Conservatory. I’ve got some live experience with local bands in my country & a few European tours as a guest performer for some Austrian Rock/Metal bands during 2007-2010. I don’t do drugs/smoke. I was somehow away from music during 2011-2013 and I lost all my repertoire and compositions, recordings etc… I learn fast by Note, Tab or ear, so learning new songs is not a problem. I’ve had my own home studio (who doesn’t?!) for a few years and I’ve got a little experience in recording stuff. This is the list of my current Guitars & Amps/FX:

Ibanez RG570EX (Custom wiring & Seymour Duncan pickups)

Ibanez Prestige RG3120 (Dimarzio pickups)

Ibanez Prestige RG1527 7-Strings (Dimarzio pickups)

Godin Multiac Jazz Semi-Hollow

Greg Bennett Classical Cutaway

ENGL SE E670 EL34 100W + ENGL XXL 4x12 Cab (No I don’t have 2 cabs right now unfortunately)

Marshall AVT-100 Combo

Fractal Audio AxeFX-II XL+

Boss DS-1, MT-2, Crybaby Wah Pedal 535Q

I’m planning to get more FX & Guitars a soon as I earn enough money! Well, I’m currently living in Redmond, Washington, working 3 days of 18 hours rounds in a week, also study IS- Software Development at Bellevue College, Full-time. But I really can’t take it anymore, I need to spend my time on things that I have love & passion for which is Music… I have my driver’s license and a truck, clean driving & criminal record. I would love to hear your music and talk more about everything. If you have any questions or need more information, please don’t hesitate to ask by email or simply call me.

Thanks for reminding me that I’m a Musician, not a Robot.

Edwin
